Fantasy

Genre Color: Pink

These are stories that feature major fantasy elements such as: magic, fanciful creatures, prophecies, mythology, and make-believe.



Inkling

Interest Level: 3rd-6th Grade
Written by Kenneth Oppel; Illustrated by Sydney Smith
2021-2022 Nominee for the Maud Hart Lovelace Award

When an inkblot, who can write, listen, learn, and draw, jumps out of Mr. Rylance's sketchbook, Ethan believes he may be the answer to their problems and names him Inkling.



Wings of Fire: The Dragonet Prophecy

Interest Level: 3rd-6th Grade
Written by Tui Sutherland
Part of the Wings of Fire series

Determined to end a generations-long war among the seven dragon tribes, a secret movement called the Talons of Peace draws on a prophecy that calls for a great sacrifice, compelling five appointed dragonets to fulfill a painful destiny against their will.



Artemis Fowl

Interest Level: 5th-8th Grade
Written by Eoin Colfer
Part of the Artemis Fowl series

When a twelve-year-old evil genius tries to restore his family fortune by capturing a fairy and demanding a ransom in gold, the fairies fight back with magic, technology, and a particularly nasty troll.



Dragon Masters: Rise of the Earth Dragon

Interest Level: K-3rd Grade
Written by Tracey West; Illustrated by Graham Howells
Part of theDragon Masters series

Snatched up by a royal soldier and carried away to the castle, 8-year-old Drake trains along with three other children to become Dragon Masters who must discover their assigned dragons' special powers.



Rainbow Magic: Ashley the Dragon Fairy

Interest Level: K-3rd Grade
Written by Daisy Meadows
Part of the Rainbow Magic series

When Rachel and Kirsty explore the cool caves at the outdoor adventure camp, they discover some of Jack Frost's goblins, who are searching for Ashley the dragon fairy's missing magical pet.



Unicorn Diaries: Bo's Magical New Friend

Interest Level: K-3rd Grade
Written by Rebecca Elliot
Part of the Unicorn Diaries series

A debut entry in a new series by the best-selling creator of the Owl Diaries finds wish-granting unicorn Rainbow Tinseltail longing for a best friend before meeting newcomer Sunny Huckleberry, who has not yet discovered his unicorn powers. Simultaneous. Illustrations.
