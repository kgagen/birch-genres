Birch Lake Genre Guide

A brief overview of the nine new genres in the Birch Lake Media Center.


ADVENTURE

Genre Color: Blue

These are stories featuring action, adventure, fast-paced plots, high-stakes situations, and survival. Characters may go on epic journeys where risk and danger are everywhere to achieve their goals.


ANIMALS

Genre Color: Red

These stories feature animals as the main characters or have central plots revolving around animals.


FANTASY

Genre Color: Pink

These are stories that feature major fantasy elements such as: magic, fanciful creatures, prophecies, mythology, and make-believe.


HISTORICAL FICTION

Genre Color: Yellow

These stories take place in a particular time period in the past. The settings are based on historical events such as wars, world history, etc. but often the characters are fictional.


HORROR

Genre Color: Orange

These stories feature supernatural elements like ghosts, monsters, and other paranormal creatures with creepy and scary plots.


Mystery

Genre Color: Dark Green

These stories revolve around mysterious events which are not explained and crimes that are not solved until the end of the story to keep the reader in suspense. Stories are thrilling and lead readers along to follow the clues or help solve the mystery.


REALISTIC FICTION

Genre Color: Light Green

These stories take place in modern times and realistic settings. The characters are involved in events that could really happen, and often feature plots about everyday problems like school, family, friendship, etc.


SCIENCE FICTION (SCI-FI)

Genre Color: Purple

Stories that revolve around futuristic technology, travel through space and time, future societies, aliens, dystopias, etc


SPORTS FICTION

Genre Color: Dark Red / Maroon

These stories revolve around team or individual sports, training for sports, and have major themes about sportsmanship.
