ABOUT THIS WEBSITE

In 2022, Birch Lake Elementary School's media center will be reorganized so that books with similar content will be grouped together in book categories called genres. We will have nine new genres (book categories) that will help everyone find more books that interest them!

This website provides the information you need to understand WHY we will be using genres in the media center, WHAT our new book genres are, and how to find books by genre IN THE MEDIA CENTER or IN DESTINY DISCOVER.

USING THIS WEBSITE

To navigate this website, use the menu bar that is dark blue with orange letters at the top of this page to see the different sections of the website. If you hover your cursor over GENRES & RECOMMENDATIONS and LOCATING BOOKS BY GENRE, you will see a dropdown menu for more pages, including a page of recommendations for each new genre!

If you want to come back to this page, you can click the "HOME" button on the menu or click the image at the top of the page. You may also use the menu on the left side to quickly access each page and see book recommendations.
